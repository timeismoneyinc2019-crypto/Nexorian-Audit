Governance & Determinism

The Law Envelope

At the center of Nexorian governance is the Law Envelope. Unlike standard AI alignment that relies on text-based prompts or post-hoc filters, the Law Envelope is a cryptographic boundary that wraps the execution kernel from the bottom up.

Admissibility Logic

1
Input Validation: Data enters via the Admissibility Gate.
2
Cryptographic Check: The Law Envelope verifies the instruction against predefined deterministic bounds.
3
Deterministic Execution: Instruction is processed by the Kernel only if admissible=True.
4
Immutable Audit: The state transition is logged to the SHA3-256 ledger.

Audit Trails

Every execution step is logged and verifiable. In the event of an investigation or regulatory audit, Nexorian provides a full chain of state hashes, proving that the system operated within its legal mandate at every Epoch.
